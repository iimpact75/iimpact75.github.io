Design Principles

Steven Philips

Visual Heirarchy

Nintendo's Zelda.com

Nintendo's has done a great job with their pages. They aren't only beautiful, but they are responsice as well for a wonderful viewing experience no matter what device you are viewing with. They stick to good desing principles. The design principle here is the "Visual Heirarchy". Notice how the character Link is prodominate figure since he is the hero in the Zelda saga.

White Space

Tabasco.com

Tabasco has been keeping things simple for over 150 years. The website is simple and responsive. What makes the Tabasco webpage delightful is the use of white space. The menu isn't colored and the space around the text is ample. The eye has nice resting points as they get the core of the message across.

Contrast

Craftsman.com

Craftsman has always been bold so why would the website be any different. The use of contrast for their color selection is what draws a viewer in.
