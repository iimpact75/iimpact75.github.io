Site Name

Weather is the answer to the life, the universe, and everything. It is the one thing that anyone can start talking about to break the ice. The website name is 6by9Weather.com since it answers to 42.

Site Purpose

Weather affects all of us for every in every aspect of our lives. Weather impacts our safety and survival. Knowing the weather can help us prepare or adjust plans to keep in line with our safety and comfort if we need to be in the weather. The purpose of this site is to keep the local sites weather as these sites are micro climates and exhibit extreme weather patters differently to the region weather. The weather site must be quick to navigate with ease of finding which communicaty local the user wishes to see. Tourists will also be likely visitor as the outdoor recreations tourism is particularly high in these areas.
