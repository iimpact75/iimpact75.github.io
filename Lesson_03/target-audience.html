General

There are 2 different target audiences. The first is the local redsidents and companies that are vested in the area. These are the business owner, parents, employees that need to navigate through the city as part of life.
The other typs is the tourist that will be visiting for a short time. These tourists are what support the local business so it is proposed to attract active youth and adults (ages 16-50) that have a middle income bracket or higher.

Target Personas

Persona 1 - Tourists

The area will have a large numbers of tourist in all seasons depending upon the outdoor activity.

Summer activits include:
  • Fishing
  • Hiking
  • Mountain biking
  • Camping
  • Picnicing
Winter activities include:
  • Skiing
  • Hunting
  • Snow-mobiling
  • Ice Fishing

For this reason the weather has to be current and accurate.

Persona 2 - Local Residents

Local residents include companies and city officials. Weather for them is important as it impacts the commutte around the different towns. Street conditions can rapidly change from backed up to clogged to dangerous depending on the weather. Certain roads have been neglected and are impassible during large rains as drains back up onto the streets as an example. It is imperative that the locals know the weather on a moments notice.

Scenarios

Scenario 1 - What is the weather at the best fishing hole?

Weather affects the fishing if you are fishing in the stream or lake. Weather causes fish to act differently if it is cloudy or sunny as this affects their eating habits.

Scenario 2 - Will the roads be impacted by the weather?

Locals know the area well and what the weather can mean to their commutes. The incramental weather in this area can be sporadic and thus real-time accurate data is required.

Scenario 3 - What is the weather like for my activity so I can gear up properly?

Most outdoor activites are dependent upon the weather. This impacts what a person may need to pack with them for a trip to their activity in these areas that are covered by this weather service.

Scenario 4 - Will I need snow chains for my winter activities?

Gear is one thing and equipment is another. Outfitting vehicles with the right equipment is essential in these area from season to season. This weather site ought to assist the site visitor to know what to prepare for safety.

Scenario 5 - How hot will it get at those elevations?

Weather changes with the elevations that each city is at. Weather also impacts breathing conditions and could be a concern for those with health issues that may need to avoid certain activities in the extreme heat or even extreme cold.
